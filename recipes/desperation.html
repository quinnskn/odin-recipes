Desperation

This recipe saved me in college when I was totally out of funds--thus the name of it! As long as you have rice, you can include as many or as few of the other ingredients as you want.

  • Rice
  • Beans of choice
  • Chicken
  • Cream of mushroom
  • Rotel
  1. Cook rice.
  2. Mix rice and all other ingredients in a 9x13 pan. Cover with cheese if desired.
  3. Bake at 350 for fifteen to twenty minutes.
