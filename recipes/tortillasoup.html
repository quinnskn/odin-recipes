Tortilla Soup

This is a very versatile soup that you can largely put anything in, as long as you stick to the four pillars of cooking: Heat, Salt, Fat, and Acid.

  • Chicken
  • Rotel
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Beans of choice
  • Chili crisp
  • chicken broth
  1. Cube the chicken, then throw in large pot to sear.
  2. Combine rest of ingredients in pot.
  3. Add seasonings as desired; note that it'll taste better the longer you let it sit.
